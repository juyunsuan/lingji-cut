# Lingji Jianying v1.3 Launch Film

## Style Prompt
Premium software launch film for a local-first AI video workstation. The visual language is cinematic, product-led, and credible: dark studio canvas, large Chinese typography, sharp real screenshots, precise camera movement, blue system highlights, and clean workflow diagrams. It should feel like a serious creator tool has matured into a complete production system.

## Colors
- Studio Black: `#08090B`
- Panel Black: `#15171C`
- Product Blue: `#0A84FF`
- Warm Signal: `#FFB86B`
- Primary Text: `#F6F8FB`
- Secondary Text: `#B7C0CE`
- Hairline: `rgba(255,255,255,0.16)`

## Typography
- Display: `SF Pro Display`, `PingFang SC`, `-apple-system`, sans-serif
- Body: `SF Pro Text`, `PingFang SC`, `-apple-system`, sans-serif
- Data: `SF Mono`, `Menlo`, monospace

## Motion
- Product screenshots move like dimensional panels in a studio.
- Use vertical light wipes as the primary transition and one punchy overexposure reveal for the publishing climax.
- Text enters with confident short pushes, not bouncy motion.

## What NOT To Do
- No playful stickers or emoji.
- No purple/cyan neon domination.
- No generic SaaS card grid.
- No cluttered feature table.
- No exposure of private project content from the current running app.
